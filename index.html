Mozilla

The Mozilla project is a global community of technologists, thinkers, and builders working together to keep the Internet alive and accessible, so people everywhere can be informed contributors and creators of the Web. We believe this act of human collaboration across an open platform is essential to the individual growth and the future of our collective digital life.

Read the Mozilla Manifesto to learn even more about the values and principles that guide our pursuit of a healthy Internet for all.
